What I Do

A list of some cool projects

Chicago Elections Project

Databridge@VT is trying to create a web application allowing the general public to visualize Chicago's historic election (1880 - 2018).

Bidentate Drug Discovery

In conjunction with the Lowell Lab, The Bevan & Brown Lab is modeling chemically linked pairs of antibiotics. We hope these bidentate antibiotics prove to be effective longer against rapidly evolving bacteria.

TeraThink RFP-Shredder

Working with a team of IScholars students, I've been given the opportunity to work with TeraThink. In this project, my team is trying to analyze Requests-For-Proposals and determine if written Responses fully address the RFP.
